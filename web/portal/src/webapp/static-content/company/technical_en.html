BERGLAND IT is a company, looking to provide 'product-like' web solutions to their customers and/or resellers.

We are working closely with the eXo Platform team, to provide 'plug-and-play' web components that offer added value to the customer, such as the Bergland Form Portlet, which will enable developers to easily create rich, interactive input Forms with a minimum of effort, or the Bergland Job Service and Portlets, which will enable us and our resellers to deliver high quality web applications to our customers, at a minimum cost.

We selected the eXo Platform because we have found, after an extensive testing period with platforms from other vendors, that the eXo platform offers a very high value-to-cost ratio, combined with high compatibility and openness, making it easy to use in combination with products from other vendors.

The quality of the individual components is excellent in itself, and the platform as a whole is very well thought and balances the needs for compatibility with those for productivity in an excellent way, making it more than just the sum of it's parts.

We were especially impressed by the design of the navigation service.

We have found that navigation is an oft-overlooked area of portal platforms design, but not so with the eXo Platform. And, apart of cost and quality, the eXo platform is Open Source, which for us, was a must.

PSWinCom™ SMS Gateway is a professional, commercial solution for SMS communication worldwide. It is based on open standards such as XML, HTTP(S), SMTP and SOAP.

The PSWinCom SMS Gateway Service has no setup fee or monthly fee and operating through a network of operators thus always ensuring the best delivery rate and quality.

PSWinCom endorses open source portal initiatives such as the eXo platform™.

XWiki is a powerful and extensible open-source Wiki engine written in Java. It features a complete wiki content management engine, including access control, version control and database storage. It also includes a powerful object-oriented database model and a programming API allowing to develop data-oriented applications integrated with the Wiki engine.

XWiki has been integrated as an add on to eXo Platform providing real-time editing of content within the Portal environment and is the first Wiki to be fully integrated with eXo Platform. The integration includes authentication and group management, navigation integration and access to eXo Platform services from the XWiki programming API.
