The eXo platform SARL is a privately held company that was created in November 2003 to provide services and support for the popular OpenSource project, eXo platform. The company headquarters are located at the 92 rue Charles Chefson, 92270 Bois Colombes, France.

The eXo platform SARL covers North America and Europe on a direct basis. We provides coverage worldwide via our growing authorized partner network.

Our goal is to provide portal platform technology that offers the lowest cost of ownership via open source software licenses that are backed up by expert technical support services. We aim to be the safe choice for enterprises and software providers alike.
