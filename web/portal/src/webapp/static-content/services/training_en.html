The eXo platform SARL offers two types of training - the e-learning program and the on-site program - as a package or as separate components.

The on-line e-learning is based on the use of the communication tools provided with the eXo platform.

  • The on-line training. Thanks to the platform's synchronous and asynchronous tools the employees/users communicate directly with the teacher.
  • Interactive programs. We are also developing a set of video clips and interactive programs to be used by employees and the company's Extranet users for training purposes. These tools complete the default help tools provided with the platform.

In addition to our e-learning services, we provide on-site education and training performed by an eXo platform certified consultant. This program is organised as a set of class courses over one or two weeks.

  • Basic course. The basic training course covers how to use the different tools and help portlets.
  • Advanced course. The advanced training course covers personalising of the user's platform layout and contents, of the business processes related to the user's work, and integration of third party applications into the platform.

With these extensive training services the eXo platform SARL provides flexible professional accompaniment in your employees' adaptation.
