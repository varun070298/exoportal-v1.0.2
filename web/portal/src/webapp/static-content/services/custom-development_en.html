The eXo platform SARL can perform any additional developments suiting your specific requirements, whether you have used our set of services to introduce the eXo platform™ into your company or not. We offer two types of custom development:

  • The on-line custom development. Representing the developers' knowledge of the platform, we can execute some developments without sending our team on-site. At all stages of the development - from the detailed requirements specification to the final integration - we collaborate with your team via synchronous and asynchronous communication tools on the platform.
  • The on-site custom development. This mode is generally used for more value added developments where we send a team of eXo platform™ certified consultants to work on-site. In this case, the consultants also collaborate with your technical team through all devlopment stages, but your team contributions and responsabilities are of less importance.
