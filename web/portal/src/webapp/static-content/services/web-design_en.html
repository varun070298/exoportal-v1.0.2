The web site design is essential for a good corporate portal. It must adhere to the company's graphic charter, and most important, it must reflect the corporate values. This way every user - whether an employee or a client - can identify with these values and abide by them. Therefore, the eXo platform SARL provides a professional web design service based on the eXo platform™ performed in two successive stages:

Design build-up. First the target colours are determined, reflecting the company's cultural environment - nationality, business sector, etc. The company's graphic charter is then used to define the site's general layout and appearance. This work is based on marketing studies and is performed in colaboration with the company's marketing division.

Development. Based on the graphic charter and policies established during the first stage our design team carries out further necessary developments:

  • Color. The colors are defined according to the client's requirements.
  • Portlets design.The portlets' colors, layout and overall design are adapted to the graphic charter.
  • Banner, footer and columns design. The banner, footer or columns can be deleted from some of the pages. Their size, colour and graphic elements are adapted to the specified requirements.
  • Buttons and icons. Special buttons and icons are designed or selected from the catalogue.

Our web design service naturally complements our portal offering. Indeed, thanks to our extensive knowledge of the eXo platform™ , we provide a fast and reliable web design service up to the highest professional standards, for a price way below the industry average.
